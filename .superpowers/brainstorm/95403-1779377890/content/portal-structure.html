Client Portal — Navigation Structure

40+ processes, clients may have multiple. How should the portal be organized?

A

Process-first (one dashboard per process)

Client logs in → sees list of their processes → clicks one → lands on that process's full dashboard. Each process is completely self-contained: its own KPIs, glide paths, action plans, governance checklist.

My Processes
▶ Airtel Inbound Support  ● On Track
▶ Airtel Outbound Sales   ● At Risk
▶ Airtel QA Audit        ● Off Track

Best when client wants to deep-dive one process at a time. Clean and focused.

B

Client Overview + Process drill-down

Client logs in → sees a summary scorecard across ALL their processes → clicks any metric tile to drill into that process. Top-level view shows combined health, then drill-down for details.

Best when client has 3+ processes and wants a single pulse view. Suits CXO-level client contacts.

Recommended

Option B — clients with 1 process get taken straight to it (no extra click). Clients with 3+ processes get the overview. One portal, both work naturally. The overview scorecard also becomes a powerful conversation starter in client review calls.
